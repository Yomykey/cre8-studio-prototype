Meet The Founder

DAN RICHARDSON

CRE8 Studio is a professional recording, podcast and content creation studio based in Cannock, Staffordshire, and the creative home of producer & musician Dan Richardson.

On Stage

With over 20 years of experience in the music industry, Dan has built his career both on stage and behind the scenes. Having performed across a wide range of genres, he has shared stages with major artists including Enter Shikari and Royal Blood, gaining invaluable experience in live performance, songwriting and audience engagement.

In The Studio

Alongside his work as a musician, Dan has spent years developing his skills as a producer and engineer. His productions have amassed over 1 million streams across major platforms and have received multiple features on BBC Radio 1 & BBC Introducing. He has also become a regular feature across several BBC Radio stations, discussing music, production and the creative process.

"Today, Dan combines his passion for music, audio and content creation to help artists, businesses and creators bring their ideas to life — whether that's a podcast, a single, video content, or a project built from the ground up."

The combination of two decades of live performance, studio production and experience across multiple genres has shaped Dan into a creative at heart — someone who understands not only how great content is made, but how to make it connect with an audience.
